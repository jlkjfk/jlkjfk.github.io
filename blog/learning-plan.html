Learning-plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

My long-time goal is to have a career that provides me with the flexibility of time and working location
and also allows me to apply my creativity to the projects.
I understand that working as an IT professional can be challenging and requires lifelong learning, however,
the achievement of task completion is a huge motivation to me, which makes me feel enjoyable.

A description of your strengths and limitations concerning learning.

strengths:

I am a self-motivated person. In terms of learning, I am curious and passionate about learning new knowledge.
Also, when I am struggle with any issue, I will not give up. Instead, I always try different ways to figure out the best solution.
I am also a highly focused person. I can always pay attention to the details and concentrate on my tasks which help me to achieve my goal efficiently.

limitations:

To be always perfect, on the other hand, could limit my performance. Sometime, I may be struggle about if I need to jump out of my comfort zone
to challenge myself doing what I am not that good at.
The other limitation is that it can take time for me to bravely express my idea and my concern when working in a group.
I know communication is important; however, a fear of facing conflict and being shy of expressing myself are the main barrier for me to get close to people.
This has an impact on my learning, especially when discussing a topic with my classmates or asking questions through Discord.

Over the past few year, I get to know that learning from process can be more meanful than a perfect result. I think that is also why reflection is so important.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

One of the skills I would like to build up is keeping confident when I am under pressure.
As I have mentioned, I am a perfectionist, I used to put too much pressure on myself to achieve my goal perfectly.
I may self-question whether there might be a better one to achieve my goal. However, during the course, I would like to build up my confidence when facing pressure.
I think it is even more important to solidify my mind when facing pressure.

The other skill I would like to develop is to be more extravert when facing an issue.
I felt shy and was afraid of asking for help when I got stuck on a question. I want to develop the skill of asking for help when needed.
No question is a silly question when people are learning a new skill; therefore, I need to be braver next time to yell out for the help.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I will treat this program as a full-time job, and work on Sat/Sun to catch up if I’m behind.
I will discuss the issue with some of my fellow students and will also reach out to my facilitator if I’m struggling.
If things start building up and are starting to get to me I will take a break to refresh my brain
and take a step back to reflect on what I am struggled with and how I could solve the issue.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will try to research the online resources such as google and YouTube.
I will use Discord to search for the answers to the questions which are similar to mine.
If there is no similar question post on Discord, I will raise my questions and I will be very open-minded to discuss any responses.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

The facilitation team is doing a great job and sometimes I even see replies from them after hours.
It could be helpful if they can share some personal coding experiences and how they went through the tough times.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

As I have mentioned, I treat this course as a full-time job, I block at least 8 hours of studying every day.
